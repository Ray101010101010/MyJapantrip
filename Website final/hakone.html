HAKONE

The life outside

Hakone is a retreat just outside Tokyo, famous for its hot springs, serene lakes, and stunning views of Mount Fuji. From relaxing in traditional onsens to cruising across Lake Ashi with the iconic torii gate of Hakone Shrine in view. The Hakone Ropeway offers beautiful scenerys of volcanic landscapes, while the region’s art museums and quiet streets provide a peaceful, immersive escape. Hakone is the perfect place to experience the harmony of Japan’s nature, history, and relaxation.

my personal experience

My trip to Hakone was one of the most relaxing parts of my journey through Japan. Just a short ride from Tokyo, it felt like stepping into a completely different world. What I loved most about Hakone was how it combines scenery with a sense of tradition. I took in the views of Lake Ashi with my family. Riding the pirate ship cruise across the lake was a fun and memorable experience.

No trip to Hakone is complete without an onsen (hot spring) experience. Sitting in the warm mineral waters, surrounded by mountains and crisp air, was incredibly soothing and one of my favorite memories from the trip. Between the art museums, the ropeway rides over the volcanic Owakudani Valley, Hakone gave me a perfect balance of adventure and rest.
