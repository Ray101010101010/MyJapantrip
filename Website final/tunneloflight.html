TUNNEL OF LIGHT

the gateway

The Tunnel of Light is a mesmerizing pathway where countless lights create a glowing, immersive experience, perfect for capturing the magic of Japan after dark. Walking through this radiant corridor feels like stepping into another world, with shimmering colors reflecting off every surface and creating a dreamlike atmosphere. It’s an unforgettable spot for photos, evening strolls, and simply soaking in the enchanting ambiance.

my personal experience

Walking through the Tunnel of Light in Kiyotsu Gorge was one of the most unique experiences of my trip in Japan. The moment I stepped inside, I realized this wasn’t just an ordinary tunnel, it’s a mix of art and nature. Each section of the tunnel has its own art installation, from mirrors and lights to playful shapes, all designed to make you see the gorge in a unique way. The highlight was reaching the end of the tunnel, where it opens up into a wide viewing platform with a shallow pool of water on the floor. The reflection of the mountains and sky in the water beneath my feet made the view even more sureal.

What I loved most was how calm it felt. Even with other visitors around, everyone seemed to pause and quietly take in the scenery. It’s a place that really makes you slow down and appreciate how beautiful and creative Japan can be.
